Project Overview:

As part of my internship at Integra Magna, I was tasked with redesigning the company's website. Previously known as IM Designs, the agency's old website reflected its early days with a creative but cluttered aesthetic. With the rebranding to Integra Magna, the company sought a more professional, minimalistic, and clean design that aligned with their evolved brand identity and market positioning as a mature and sophisticated design agency.

Role: UX/UI Designer

Company: Integra Magna

Duration: 6 Months

Challenges:

The main challenge was to design a website that immediately captivated visitors while providing essential information at a glance. The resort needed a balance between aesthetic appeal and functionality to promote user engagement and increase booking conversions.

Design Process:

Initial research involved analyzing competitor sites and understanding user expectations through surveys. Based on the insights gained, We developed several wireframe iterations to explore different layouts and navigation structures. Prototypes were then tested with users to refine the UX before finalizing the design.

The final design features a clean, responsive layout with a calming color palette that reflects the serenity of the resort. Key information is accessible within one click through a streamlined navigation bar. Prominent call-to-action are placed strategically to guide users towards booking inquiries, significantly enhancing user flow and accessibility while keeping the balance of aesthetics.

The website design should be strategically crafted so that it cater to four distinct traveler demographics: Adventure Seekers, Wellness Enthusiasts, Luxury Vacationers, and Eco-Conscious Travelers. Each group has unique preferences and needs that the website aims to fulfill efficiently and elegantly

Conclusion:

Overall, the website’s design prioritizes user-friendly navigation and responsive layout, ensuring accessibility and ease of use across devices. Strategic placement of call-to-action buttons enhances user flow, guiding visitors towards making booking inquiries effortlessly. This thoughtful design not only meets the specific needs of each target group but also enhances the overall user experience, balancing aesthetic appeal with functional excellence. This approach ensures that every visitor feels understood and catered to, from the moment they enter the site.
