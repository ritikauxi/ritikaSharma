Project Overview:

As part of my portfolio, I wanted to showcase my ability to create a visually compelling and user-friendly landing page for a high-end restaurant. The aim was to design a landing page that reflects the elegance and culinary excellence of a brand like Sun & Savory, making it appealing and engaging for potential customers.

Role:
Product Designer

Tools Used:
Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

Duration:
2 Months

Challenges:

  • Balancing Aesthetics and Functionality: Ensured that the landing page was visually appealing while maintaining ease of navigation and user-friendliness.
  • Content Hierarchy: Prioritized content to highlight the restaurant's unique selling points without overwhelming the user.

Design Development

  • Visual Design: Focused on a luxurious aesthetic with a dark green color palette, elegant typography, and high-quality images to convey the brand's identity.
  • Typography: Chose sophisticated typefaces to enhance readability and add a touch of class.
  • Imagery: Incorporated high-resolution images of dishes and the restaurant's ambiance to entice users.

Research and Inspiration:

Market Analysis: Studied top restaurant websites to understand current design trends and user expectations.
Mood Boards: Developed mood boards to capture the desired aesthetic of elegance and sophistication.

The website design should be strategically crafted so that it cater to four distinct traveler demographics: Adventure Seekers, Wellness Enthusiasts, Luxury Vacationers, and Eco-Conscious Travelers. Each group has unique preferences and needs that the website aims to fulfill efficiently and elegantly

Conclusion:

The conceptual landing page design for Sun & Savory successfully captures the elegance and culinary excellence of a high-end restaurant. The visual appeal, combined with intuitive navigation and engaging content, provides a seamless user experience. This project demonstrates my ability to create aesthetically pleasing and functional designs that meet the needs of both the brand and its users.

Key Takeaways
  • Design Excellence: Demonstrated the ability to balance visual appeal with usability, creating a landing page that is both beautiful and functional
  • User-Centric Approach: Emphasized the importance of understanding user needs and preferences in the design process.
